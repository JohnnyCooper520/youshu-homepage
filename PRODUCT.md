# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Younger Chinese-speaking consumers who are at a decision point in work, relationships, money, or personal direction. They are curious about Eastern metaphysics but want language that feels modern, useful, and emotionally precise rather than theatrical or superstitious.

Primary use context: a user sees a short-form post or shared result card, lands on the web page, enters birth information, and wants a quick sense of "what kind of pattern am I in, and what should I pay attention to next?"

## Product Purpose

有数 is an Eastern metaphysics AI life-advisor brand. It turns deterministic birth-chart calculation and advisor-reviewed interpretation rules into readable AI guidance: personality patterns, stage trends, decision prompts, and concrete next-step suggestions.

The product should help users feel more clear, not more dependent. Success means the first free result feels screenshot-worthy, the paid report feels specific enough to trust, and the brand earns repeat usage without fear tactics or "guaranteed accuracy" claims.

## Brand Personality

Calm, lucid, restrained.

The voice should feel like a wise modern advisor: culturally rooted, emotionally observant, and decisive when giving guidance. It can say "准" in marketing-adjacent emotional language, but formal product output, payment flows, and agreements must keep the frame as reference and decision support.

## Anti-references

Do not look or sound like a temple, incense shop, fortune-telling stall, cheap guochao poster, scammy spirituality account, horoscope content farm, or fear-based "change your fate" service.

Avoid visual dependence on candles, smoke, statues, talismans, excessive red/gold, fake parchment, over-mystified rituals, or master worship. Avoid copy that promises guaranteed outcomes, threatens bad consequences, sells magical resolution, or exploits breakup anxiety.

## Design Principles

Make the product feel like clarity, not worship. The interface should center choice, self-understanding, and pattern recognition.

Use Eastern structure without costume. Yin-yang, hexagram logic, fate versus luck, and cyclic change can guide the system, but the execution should remain modern and premium.

Let the output become the growth loop. Result cards and report fragments should be polished enough to share naturally on social platforms.

Protect trust before conversion. High-risk topics such as health, investment, legal issues, and emotional crisis need constrained guidance and professional referrals.

Keep AI as the product, with humans as quality infrastructure. The brand should not depend on a single master personality; advisor expertise is encoded into rules, prompts, and review.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for public pages and product flows. The page must remain readable in Chinese on desktop and mobile, respect reduced-motion settings, avoid content hidden behind animation, and not rely on color alone to communicate meaning.

Users may arrive in anxious or vulnerable states, so the product should avoid manipulative urgency, fear copy, and definitive life instructions. Guidance should be framed as perspective and decision support.
